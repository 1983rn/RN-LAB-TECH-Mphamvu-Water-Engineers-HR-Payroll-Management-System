# Company Header Image

Please place your company header image here as `company_header.png`.

The image should contain:
- MPHAMVU WATER ENGINEERS logo
- "Always Water" motto
- Contact information
- Professional design matching your company branding

The image will be displayed at the top of all PDF documents including:
- Payslips
- Quotations
- Invoices
- Delivery Notes

Recommended image size: 7 inches wide x 1.5 inches high (504 x 108 pixels at 72 DPI)
